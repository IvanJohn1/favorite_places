Krasnodar
Ekaterinodar

Krasnodar is the historical capital of the Kuban and the regional center in the south of Russia, the symbolic "gate" of the Caucasus. The city is located in the valley of the Kuban River and is about 150 km away from the shores of the Black and Azov Seas .
